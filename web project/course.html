EPERIENCED RIDER COURSE

        The ERC is designed for experienced
        motorcyclists who already have
        motorcycle endorsements and want to
        improve their riding skills on
        their own motorcycles.
        This half-day course will enable riders
        to hone the physical and mental skills
        necessary for surviving traffic.
